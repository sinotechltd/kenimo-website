DIGITAL MATATU WORKSHEET
The future of Matatu accounting.
Traditional data management processes make it difficult to scale capabilities without compromising governance or security. No matter how successful and well managed your Matatu is, work sheet handling has been stressful due to a lot of circumstances, the digital work sheet makes it easier to handle and manage matatu work sheets.
DIGITAL MATATU WORK SHEET

The Digital Matatu Work Sheet is a QR based web app that typically works like the normal manual work sheet. Since it’s a Qr based web app, it means each and every matatu will have its own unique QR code. The QR code is the gateway to access the work sheet form.

The process starts once the matatu has completed its trip since the sheet will be filled after every trip to give the records a timestamp. At the end of the trip the driver or conductor or stage attendant scans the matatu's Qr code to access the work sheet form. Date, Drivers name, Conductors name, type of trip and income generated during the trip are the details filled in the first page of the form.

After filling the first page of the form, one is prompted to go to the next page which is the Expenses page form.
In the Expenses page form, one is required to key in the expenses incurred during the trip.

On the admin side an Admin Dashboard will be provided where all the data collected will be showcased; all the trips timestamp, income, individual expenses incurred, Total daily income, Total daily expenses, Total monthly income, Total monthly expenses, Total annual income and Total annual expenses. The will be reports for four models which are Collection/Sales, Inventory, Purchases and Accounting.

WHY INVEST IN THIS APPLICATION

1. High Speed

Digital Matatu Work Sheet offers a great speed, helping you to do your tasks within matter of seconds. Data entry is done is done in seconds and also transmitted to the main office in seconds. (REAL TIME DATA TRANSMISSION.)

2. Accuracy

Humans make errors. Hence, the Digital Matatu Work Sheet is very accurate making it quite reliable.

3. Automation

A lot of tasks have been automated saving a lot of time. For example, instead of manually calculating some values like daily total income or annual total expenses the application is designed to complete such kind of tasks automatically. This saves a lot of time ensuring 100% accuracy.

4. Storage

Storage of work sheet papers in physical files take a lot of time and space. Digital storage of these files will save a lot of time and space.

5. Ease of Access

The ease of access of information provided by the application contributes towards saving time and efforts.

6. Better understanding of data

The Digital Matatu Work Sheet supports a lot of tools for data analysis and mining. Organizations make use of this benefit to support data analysis and visualization helpful for decision making.
