PARK & SHOP
HOW PARK & SHOP WORKS.

The system is a QR code web application that will enable shoppers who visit malls to shop from anywhere within the mall premises, shoppers could be sitting in their cars and their orders delivered to them while still in their cars.

PARK and SHOP will have all the shops within a mall integrated into the system, the shops/stalls will showcase what they are selling in the application. Selling and buying of goods will be conducted through the app.

To access the park and shop services of a mall, a shopper will scan the QR code at the entrance of the mall and will be automatically connected to the web app.

Once connected the shopper can access all the shops within the mall, the shopper can shop in any of the shops simultaneously. Payment will be done through through an popular payments api's like MPESA and VISA. Park and Shop merchandizers will receive the orders and prepare them for delivery. Delivery will be done at the parking area of the mall.

EXCITING FEATURES OF THE APP (UNIQUE FEATURES)

1. The web application will have most, if not all malls available in the app to broaden the choices for the customer.

2. Choice of supermarket/Store. The user can choose which supermarket or stall he/she wants to shop from. e.g. TUSKYS, NAIVAS or JAVA

3. Fast delivery service. Delivery will be done fast whether the Shopper is within the mall or is shopping from.

4. One can shop from two or more Shops simultaneously.


5. Choice of mode delivery. Goods maybe delivered or picked at designated pickup stations with in the mall.


6. Safe and flexible payment.
