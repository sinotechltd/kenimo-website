DIMC will combine retailtainment and technology to deliver a new and improved hospital experience. It’s a response to the growing popularity of technology in the medical field.
DIGITAL MEDICAL INSURANCE CLAIM FORM
GO GREEN GO PAPAERLESS

The main reason we came up with the idea of DIGITAL INSURANCE MEDICAL CLAIM FORM is to help with the fight against corona virus and at the same time campaign for the GO GREEN, GO PAPERLESS initiative which is advocating for institutions to go paperless.

DMIC is a mobile application that will allow patients during this pandemic period to visit their favorite hospitals with out the fear of contracting or spreading the virus.

Proponents say the DIMC will be successful because everyone wins. The consumer enjoys a more interactive and customer-focused visit while HOSPITALS AND INSURANCE FIRMS benefit from a wealth of data and have less paper work involved. The new system is set to improve the efficiency of operations of the Hospitals and insurance firms.

The application aims to improve customer experience through;

⦁ Protecting them from contracting or spreading the corona virus.
⦁ Saving time.
⦁ Avoiding long queues in the hospital.
⦁ Having a personal Ai assistant in the app will guide an individual on how to fill the forms.

BENEFITS OF DIGITAL MEDICAL INSURANCE FORM TO INSURANCE FIRMS.

⦁ RECORDS DIGITIZATION. The insurance records will be digitized, where the processes from data entry to storage will all be computerized and automated.

HOW THE APPLICATION WORKS


To use the application one simply needs to scan a QR code at any hospital,which will in turn direct you to the web application. Once connected to the application, you get to choose which insrance scheme you are using. The next step will be to key in details to the insurance form chose earlier.

The details that are keyed in are typically the same with the ones we write on paper. Upon completion submit your details. The rest of the procedure is the same before discharge of a patient.
