UHURU




LETS PROMOTE UJIRANI MWEMA & EARN




Uhuru is a social mobile web app like facebook for residents who live aroud Kimathi,Jerusalem, Uhuru, Jericho, Harambe and Buruburu.
The main purpose of the app is to connect the residents of these areas with aim of promoting Ujirani Mwema and provide more earning opportunities for the community.













APP FEATURES
1. CONNECT

Uhuru app connects the community, creates a social platform for the residents to share

2. ONLINE SHOPPING

Residents will have the conviniency of shopping from their favourite local shops at the comfort of their homes.

3. SOCIO-ECONOMIC SERVICES

Socio-economic services that are not usually online and not on other platforms will be available on the app. Services such as:

1.Mali kwa Mali
2.Mama mboga
3.Chomelea
4.Plumbing Services
5.Electrical Services

4. VIDEO HUB/TALENT CENTER

Just like other social platforms users will get to post in the app and repost content to other social platforms. Talented YOUTHS will get a chance to show case their gifts on the apps video hub and earn depending on the number of views and other parameters.
