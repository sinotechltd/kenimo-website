HOW INFINITE LOVE WORKS.



The system is a QR code web application that will enable graveside visitors to Connect to online memorials of those at rest,
we are creating an online presence for those who have died with help of family and friends.
1. A biography of the deceased
2. Obituary
3. Eulogy
4. Photos
5. Videos
Family and friends will be able to submit photos , videos, text messages/tributes, and audio recordings.
One will be able to access the site/profiles of the deceased by opening the site(www.infinitelove.com) from their browsers or scanning a Qr code on the headstone.
